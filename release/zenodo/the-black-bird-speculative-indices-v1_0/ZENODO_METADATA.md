**Creator:**

**Creators:**

**Description:**  
This deposit presents speculative indices developed from the object grammar of The Black Bird, a hypergraph research poem. The indices quantify how objects distribute across the research-field and how they share mediating conditions through Relation Objects.

Three index families are included: Object Incidence (OI), which records research recurrence for Research Note Objects and Field Objects; Relational Thickness (RT), which records co-incidence between object pairs through shared Research Note Objects and shared Relation Objects; and Mediational Incidence (MI), which records how strongly a Field Object is held by Relation Objects.

The corpus covers four Research Note Objects and eighteen Field Objects from the v1.0 release of The Black Bird. The index set is bounded to X = RNO ∪ FO. Relation Objects serve as mediating sites; Mapping Note Objects, Reference Objects, and Name Objects are excluded from the primary calculation.

Key finding: Black Bird and Corpse are equal in research recurrence (OI_F = 4 each), but Corpse has Mediational Incidence 9 while Black Bird has Mediational Incidence 8. The entry-object and the densest relation-body are not identical.
