Speculative Indices
for a Research-Field

Object Incidence, Relational Thickness, and further ontography in The Black Bird

This annex introduces speculative indices developed from the object grammar of The Black Bird.

Object Incidence records how Research Note Objects and Field Objects distribute recurrence across the research-field. Relational Thickness records where objects repeatedly share mediating conditions. Mediational Incidence records how strongly a Field Object is held by Relation Objects.

The indices give recurrence a surface. They open pathways for further ontography, relation-making, and poetic construction.

The calculation is bounded to source-bearing Research Note Objects and extracted Field Objects. Relation Objects remain mediating sites. Mapping Note Objects, Reference Objects, and Name Objects keep their roles outside the primary calculation.

Method

Two matrices underlie the indices.

A is the RNO–FO incidence matrix. It records which Field Objects appear in each Research Note Object. A has dimensions 4 × 18: four Research Note Objects against eighteen Field Objects. A cell is 1 if the Field Object is present in that note, 0 otherwise.

B is the FO–RelO incidence matrix. It records which Field Objects participate in each Relation Object. B has dimensions 18 × 12.

From these two matrices, six indices follow:

RNO Field Breadth
OI_N(n) = Σ_f A[n,f]
Field Object Research Recurrence
OI_F(f) = Σ_n A[n,f]
RNO–RNO Field Overlap
RT_NN = A Aᵀ
FO–FO Research Co-incidence
RT_FF^N = Aᵀ A
Mediational Incidence
MI_R(f) = Σ_r B[f,r]
FO–FO RelO-Mediated Thickness
RT_FF^R = B Bᵀ

A gives research recurrence. B gives mediational participation. A Aᵀ gives RNO–RNO field overlap. Aᵀ A gives FO–FO research co-incidence. B Bᵀ gives FO–FO RelO-mediated thickness.

Key Finding

The work begins with Black Bird as the first object-field, but the relation layer gives Corpse one more mediating site than Black Bird.

Black Bird and Corpse are equal in research recurrence: each appears in all four Research Note Objects, giving both a Field Object Research Recurrence of 4. In the Relation Object layer, however, Corpse has Mediational Incidence 9 while Black Bird has Mediational Incidence 8.

The entry-object and the densest relation-body are therefore not identical. This difference is one of the useful pressures exposed by the indices.

Field Structure
Black Bird and Corpse form the universal research spine. They are the only Field Objects appearing in all four Research Note Objects, giving both a research co-incidence of 4 and a RelO-mediated thickness of 5 — the highest in the field.
Cain / Ghurāb and Huginn / Muninn form a Black Bird / God / Corpse corridor, sharing three Field Objects across their separate traditions. This is the field's densest cross-tradition overlap.
Scald-crow and American Crows form a Black Bird / Corpse / Forensic corridor, pairing the Irish death-marking and the behavioral science notes through Forensic as their distinctive shared object.
Corpse has the highest Mediational Incidence in the field (9), appearing in nine of the twelve Relation Objects. It exceeds Black Bird by one mediating site, making it the densest relation-body despite identical research recurrence.
Window, Darkness, and Alley Light have zero RNO–FO recurrence but each holds one RelO participation. These lyric-layer objects are not absent from the field — they are differently held. A future MNO–FO matrix, rather than the RNO–FO calculation, is the appropriate frame for them.
